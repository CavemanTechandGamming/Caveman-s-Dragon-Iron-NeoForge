# Caveman's Dragon Iron — Lore

This folder holds the **canon-aligned** lore and world-building for the **Dragon Iron** mod. Use it to keep the mod’s story consistent across tooltips, future books, advancements, and content.

## Contents

- **[Complete Lore (Canon-Aligned Edition)](complete_lore.md)** — The full authoritative chronicle in one document (I–X: The End, Ender Dragon, Endermen, Lost Cities, Dragon Iron, the bridge to the Overworld, discovery of the forge, Dragon Iron Furnace, properties, Dragon Smiths).
- **[Dragon Iron — Overview](dragon_iron_overview.md)** — What Dragon Iron is, how it reaches the Overworld, what can be made, and its properties (canon-aligned).
- **[The Ender Dragon's Fire](ender_dragon_fire.md)** — Metal born of dragonfire, fireproof and durable traits, and the Dragon Iron Furnace as legacy of the End (canon-aligned).
- **[Dragon Smiths & Endermen](dragon_smiths_and_endermen.md)** — Endermen as the bridge, discovery of the forge in an End City, and the Dragon Smiths as keepers of forbidden craft (canon-aligned).
- **[Vanilla End Lore](vanilla_end_lore.md)** — The End, Ender Dragon, Endermen, and Lost Cities as the canon foundation (canon-aligned).

---

*All lore is canon-aligned: Dragon Iron is a relic of the End; the dragon's flame transforms matter; Endermen carry nuggets; the first forge was found in an abandoned End City; Dragon Smiths were adventurers who became keepers of that craft. In-game tooltips match this chronicle.*
